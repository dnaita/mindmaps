Exercise: Access Enterprise NoSQL Book

In this walkthrough exercise, you access the Enterprise NoSQL for Dummies — MarkLogic Special Edition — book.

 

  1. In a browser, navigate to: info.marklogic.com/nosql-for-dummies.
  2. See that the Enterprise NoSQL for Dummies is available for free download:


  3. If desired, fill out the form to download the book.

    Note: If you would prefer to not fill out the form, you can access the book in unit01 of your mls-fundamentals folder.
  4. Click the Read Enterprise NoSQL for Dummies link, or double-click the Enterprise_NoSQL_for_Dummies.pdf file in mls-fundamentals/unit01, to open the book.
  5. Skim through the book, noting the various topics, such as schema-agnostic, ACID, commodity hardware, scalability, high availability, replication, and enterprise-class security.
  6. If desired, watch the 3-minute enterprise NoSQL database video by Jason Hunter at www.youtube.com/watch?v=3mOCOrFQ_N0.
