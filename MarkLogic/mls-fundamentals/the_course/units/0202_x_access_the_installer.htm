Exercise: Access the Installer

In this walkthrough exercise, you first go to the downloads page for MarkLogic, to see the available installers. Then you view the installer that has previously been downloaded to your virtual machine for you.

 

  1. In a browser, navigate to developer.marklogic.com/downloads.
  2. Notice the many installers.
  3. At the top of the list, notice the Read about the Free Developer License link:


  4. Click the link.
  5. Read to see how you can acquire a free developer license:

  6. Click the browser's Back button to return to the list of installers.
  7. Realize that you could select the installer of your choice, to download it.
  8. Know that the CentOS installer has previously been downloaded to your virtual machine for you.
  9. In your virtual machine, on the desktop, double-click the cent's Home folder.
  10. Double-click the Downloads folder.
