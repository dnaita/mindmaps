Exercise: Access a White Paper, Blog, and Tutorial

In this walkthrough exercise, you access white papers, blogs, and MarkLogic tutorials.

 

  1. In a browser, navigate to www.marklogic.com/resource-type/whitepapers.
  2. Scroll down through the white papers, noting any of interest to you (e.g. Inside MarkLogic Server).
  3. If desired, click the name of a white paper, fill out the form (to receive the white paper for free), and click the Download button.
  4. In a browser, navigate to www.marklogic.com/blog.
  5. Scroll down through the blog posts, noting any of interest to you (e.g. Behind the Scenes with Samplestack).
  6. If desired, click the name of a blog post, to read it.
  7. In a browser, navigate to developer.marklogic.com/blog, to view the MarkLogic developer blog:


  8. Peruse the blog posts.
  9. If desired, click the name of a blog post, to read it.
  10. At the top of the page, click the Tutorials link:


  11. See that you are now viewing the MarkLogic tutorials.
  12. If desired, click a tutorial, to read it.
  13. Continue perusing the tutorials, as desired.
