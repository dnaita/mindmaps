Exercise: Use Search

In this walkthrough exercise, you use search in the Top Songs application, to see the many text search capabilities provided by MarkLogic.

 

  1. In a browser, navigate to mlu.marklogic.com/top-songs.
  2. Notice the facet navigation, the search field, the advanced search link, and the birthday search field.
  3. In the results area, notice the number of results, the paging links, the sort options, and the more links.
  4. Notice that the default results are the 10 most recent songs in the database.
  5. In the search field, enter seeing.
  6. See that, with MarkLogic's ability to do stemming, results are returned for see, seen, and seeing.
  7. Realize that the facets are also narrowed down.
  8. Click on the page banner, to display the facet navigation based on all of the documents in the database.
  9. In the search field, enter love.
  10. See that the facets are narrowed down to only documents that contain the word love.
  11. In the decade list, select 1960s, to further narrow down the list to songs that were a number 1 hit in the 1960s.
  12. In the artist list, select the supremes, to further narrow down the results to songs where the artist is The Supremes.
  13. In the birthday search field, enter your birthday to see what song was #1 the week you were born.
  14. Click the advanced search link.
  15. See that you could do a very specific search.
  16. Continue perusing, as desired.
