Exercise: View On-Demand Training

In this walkthrough exercise, you view free on-demand training provided by MarkLogic University.

 

  1. In a browser, navigate to mlu.marklogic.com/ondemand.
  2. Scroll down to view the concepts listed in the lower-left navigation.
  3. Click the More link, to see more of the concepts listed.
  4. Click a concept that looks interesting to you (e.g. Content Pump).
  5. Click the name of an on-demand video that looks interesting to you (e.g. MarkLogic Content Pump).
  6. Wait for the video to start downloading.
  7. Start watching the video.
  8. In a browser, navigate to www.marklogic.com/resource-type/recordings.
  9. Scroll down through the list of webinars, noting ones of interest to you.
  10. If desired, click the name of one of the recordings (e.g. When Relational Databases Aren't Enough) to fill out the form to access the recording.
  11. In a browser, navigate to www.marklogic.com/webinars to view upcoming webinars.
  12. Scroll down through the list of events, noting upcoming webinars of interest to you.
  13. If desired, register to watch an upcoming webinar.
