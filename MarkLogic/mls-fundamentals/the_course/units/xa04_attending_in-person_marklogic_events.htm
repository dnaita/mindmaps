Attending In-Person MarkLogic Events

Meetups and conferences are a great way to continue learning while meeting with fellow MarkLogic gurus.

Meetups

MarkLogic Meetup groups can be located by going to marklogic.meetup.com.

If there's not already a MarkLogic group in your area, consider setting one up!

MarkLogic World

MarkLogic World is a yearly conference that brings together MarkLogic developers from all over the world.

Information on the next upcoming MarkLogic World conference is available at world.marklogic.com.

Links to recordings of the most recent MarkLogic World sessions are available at www.marklogic.com/resource-event/mlw-2015.
