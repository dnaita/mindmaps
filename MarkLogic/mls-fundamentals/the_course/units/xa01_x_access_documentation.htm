Exercise: Access Documentation

In this walkthrough exercise, you access the MarkLogic documentation.

 

  1. In a browser, navigate to docs.marklogic.com.
  2. Scroll down to see the many documentation guides available, noting ones of interest to you.
  3. Click on the name of one that interests you (e.g. Administrator's Guide).
  4. Notice the chapters listed on the page, as well as in the left navigation:


  5. Click on the PDF icon, in the upper-right.
  6. See that the guide opens as a PDF file.
  7. Click a topic of interest in the table of contents, to read about it.
  8. Continue perusing the documentation guides, as desired.
